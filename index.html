Process

Three sessions. One build. No lost motion.

1

Field Call – 60–90 min

We walk your world: who you serve, what you’re building, what you refuse to do. You talk messy. I translate it into a clean spine.

Outcome: identity spine, tone map, rough layout sketch.

2

Draft World – async + feedback

I build the first version of your space. You get a live link, walk through it, and tell me what feels true, off, or missing.

Outcome: revised structure, locked visuals, final copy pass.

3

Launch Build – 1 focused burst

I finish the page in one concentrated build window and deliver the full package to you: files, notes, and a simple “how to update this yourself” guide.

Outcome: deployed page + ZIP you fully own.
